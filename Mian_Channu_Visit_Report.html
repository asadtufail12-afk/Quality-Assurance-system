📋 EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

A joint site inspection was conducted to evaluate the progress of the ongoing renovation at Alkhidmat Jamal Hospital. While structural work is advancing, several critical finishing, electrical, and clinical infrastructure tasks remain outstanding. The following points require immediate attention to meet the operational deadline.

🧪 1. Clinical & Specialized Areas

1
Laboratory HIGH PRIORITY Civil and electrical works are currently incomplete; these must be prioritized for equipment installation. PENDING
2
X-Ray Room Installation of specialized door is still pending. PENDING
3
Pharmacy Construction of storage racks is incomplete. PENDING
4
Nursing Station HIGH PRIORITY A dedicated station on the First Floor has yet to be established/constructed. NOT STARTED

⚡ 2. Electrical & IT Infrastructure

1
Data Networking HIGH PRIORITY No networking sockets were found in any pre-identified areas. NOT DONE
2
Power Backup (UPS) The UPS line distribution needs to be addressed and routed to the identified critical areas. PENDING

🔨 3. Civil, Woodwork & Finishing

Doors & Partitioning

1
Female Ward Missing entrance door. PENDING
2
Private Rooms Repair of existing doors is pending; a separation/partition door is also required for the private area corridor. PENDING
3
Third Entrance Door closer installation and marble flooring work remain unfinished. PENDING

Woodwork & Finishing

4
General Woodwork HIGH PRIORITY General cabinetry and woodwork are lagging across the entire hospital facility. BEHIND SCHEDULE
5
Paint & Ceiling Final coat of paint is pending in multiple zones. Ceiling panels/surfaces require thorough cleaning of construction dust and debris. PENDING

💡 RECOMMENDATIONS

  • Relocation of X-Ray Room with Laboratory - Consider co-locating for better workflow and resource sharing
  • Install CCTV on the First Floor - Essential for patient safety and security monitoring
  • CSSD Area Modifications - Small work required to separate dirty and clean entrances to maintain infection control
